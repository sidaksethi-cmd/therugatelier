Our Principles

Built on Three
Uncompromising Pillars

01

Heritage Craft

Every piece is rooted in the traditions of master weavers who have passed their knowledge across generations. We honour these techniques by preserving them in every knot.

02

Singular Materials

We source only the finest natural fibres — hand-combed wools, undyed silks, plant-based pigments. Quality is non-negotiable, from raw material to finished piece.

03

Timeless Design

Our aesthetic sits at the intersection of old-world pattern and modern restraint — pieces that evolve with a space and outlast every trend they encounter.
